Overview

The PiBot Web Installer is a static web application that runs entirely in your browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy and have designed this tool to operate without collecting any personal information.

Information We Collect

None. This installer:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not use cookies or tracking
  • Does not store any data on servers
  • Does not log flash attempts or errors
  • Does not transmit any information to third parties

How It Works

The firmware flashing process happens entirely within your browser using the Web Serial API. Your browser communicates directly with your Pibot device over USB. No data passes through our servers or any third-party services.

Local Storage

The only data stored locally is your language preference, saved in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

This installer uses the open-source ESPTool.js library, which is hosted locally alongside the installer. No external CDN or third-party services are loaded when using this tool.
